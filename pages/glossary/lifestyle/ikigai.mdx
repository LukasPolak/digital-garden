# Ikigai

Ikigai is a Japanese concept that roughly translates to "reason for being." It is a belief that everyone has something that gives their life meaning and purpose and that finding this purpose is essential to leading a fulfilling and happy life. The concept of ikigai is often represented as a Venn diagram, with four circles representing the intersection of four elements:

- What you love.
- What you are good at.
- What the world needs, and what you can be paid for.

Finding one's ikigai is seen as the sweet spot at the intersection of these elements. It is often associated with the idea of being passionate about what one does and finding a career that aligns with one's values and interests.
